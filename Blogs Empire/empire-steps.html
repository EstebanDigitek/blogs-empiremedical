Information about Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19)

Empire is continually monitoring the progression of COVID-19. We remain steadfast in our sincere commitment to ensure the safety and service to all of our Empire members, faculty, employees and vendors. We encourage everyone to get their information on the COVID-19 from their local and federal health authorities.

Advice to the general public for Coronavirus is:

  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
  • Stay home when you are sick.
  • Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces using a regular household cleaning spray or wipe.
  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after going to the bathroom; before eating; and after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing.
